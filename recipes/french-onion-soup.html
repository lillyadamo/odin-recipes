French Onion Soup


Description

Josh makes this the best

Ingredients

For soup

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cups sliced onions
  • 5 cups beef broth
  • 2 tbsp dry sherry
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 pinch of salt and pepper to taste

For assembly

  • 4 slices french bread
  • 4 slices provolone cheese
  • 2 slices swiss cheese, diced
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesean

Instructions

  1. Melt butter with olive oil in a stock pot over medium heat Add onions and continually stir until tender and translucent. Do not brown the onions
  2. Add beef broth, sherry and thyme. Season with salt and pepper. Let simmer for 30 minutes
  3. Preheat the broiler
  4. Ladle soup into oven-safe serving bowls. Break up bread into chunks and place onto soup. Layer the top with provolone, swiss, and parmesean cheese
  5. Place bowls into preheated oven until cheese bubbles and browns slightly
